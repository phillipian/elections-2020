Joe Biden received overwhelming support from Andover students in a survey conducted by The Phillipian, vol. CXLIII about the upcoming presidential race. Of the 1,181 students surveyed, 65.1 percent responded and completed the survey. Biden received 78.8 percent of the votes, compared to 16.6 percent for Donald Trump. Jorgensen and Hawkins each received 0.4 percent of support.
